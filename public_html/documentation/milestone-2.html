Recipe Book/Blog

This is another website I found to be interesting, and exciting to make. I love to cook more than anything. Food is life, and for a food appreciator this website will come in handy. I am not an expert when it comes to cooking, but I do appreciate learning different recipes, especially family recipes. I also love learning different types of kitchen and cooking tips, and tricks. If you've ever been on a food blog to obtain a recipe you will have noticed that you have to get over a mountain of storytelling to get to the recipe. That's when my idea comes in, I would like to keep that aspect of food blogs, but without having to spend 6 hours reading the same thing over and over again for one recipe. The website will put the recipe first; it would also have links on how to do certain things like knife tips and tricks, how-to-instructions, etc.. Then at the end limit the story behind how you created the dish. I would also put in a "critique" area, where others like myself can give helpful tips, or opinions on the dish.

---------

Purpose:

To help, and encourage those who appreciate food to continue cooking, and learning how to make healthy, exquisite meals at home.

---------

Audience:

The target audience would be for fellow food-lovers, food critics, and people who love to cook. I do think this website will be tailored to everyone, not just one small circle of people.
